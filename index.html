About the Department of Agriculture

Mandate, Mission, and Public Service Commitment

The Department of Agriculture leads the country’s efforts in developing a modern, inclusive, and resilient agriculture and fisheries sector. It supports farmers, fisherfolk, and agribusiness stakeholders through policy direction, technical assistance, and essential government services.

This HRIS portal strengthens internal operations by helping DA personnel deliver faster, more transparent, and citizen-focused public service.

Vision

A food-secure and resilient Philippines with empowered agricultural communities.

Mission

Enable sustainable growth through responsive governance and innovation.
